About Wolf Industries and Design

Wolf industries and Design is a graphic design company that specializes in creating unique and custom vinyl stickers as well as business cards, if you think it we will do our best to design it. The company is run and co-owned by Dylan Oosthuysen and Jolanda Oosthuysen a husband and wife Duo, it was started in the early months of 2021 and has been in the process of growing ever since. Dylan Oosthuysen a qualified electrician started the company by doing hand engraved night lights as a hobby and a few small electrical jobs, he was then joined by Jolanda Oosthuysen who has her degree in Graphic and advertising design, who assisted him in the design of the night lights. Wolf Industries and Design was started with the aim to provide a wide spectrum of services and strive to do so with professionalism, enthusiasm and dedication. One of our primary goals is to make the design process itself enjoyable for our clients and expand their creative ideas by making them a reality. We have recently started delving into more digital, greener markets, and are proud to say we are launching Wolf Industries and design hosted digital Business cards, and we also dipping our toes in the mud with installation of LED Light Bars on request. Our focus on the future is to supply the business market in the region as well as in the world with our outstanding design solutions that would grow together the firm and our clients’ success..
