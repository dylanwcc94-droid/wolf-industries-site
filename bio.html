About Wolf Industries and Design

Wolf Industries and Design is dedicated to creating professional, visually balanced layouts and innovative digital solutions. Our focus is on accessibility, responsive design, and user‑friendly experiences across all platforms.

Wolf Industries and Design is an all‑in‑one company that specializes in creating unique and custom vinyl stickers as well as business cards. If you think it, we will do our best to design it. The company is run and co‑owned by a husband and wife team. It was started in the early months of 2021 and has been growing ever since.

Dylan Oosthuysen, a qualified electrician, started the company by doing hand‑engraved night lights as a hobby and a few small electrical jobs. He was later joined by his wife, Jolanda Oosthuysen, who has a degree in Graphic and Advertising Design. She assisted him in the design of the night lights.

They began with the aim of providing a wide spectrum of services and strive to do so with professionalism, enthusiasm, and dedication. One of their primary goals is to make the design process itself enjoyable for clients and expand creative ideas by making them a reality. Their focus for the future is to supply the business market in the region with outstanding design solutions that grow both the company and their clients’ success.

The company combines creativity with technical precision to deliver modern digital business cards and design services tailored to client needs.

We believe in innovation, professionalism, and making technology accessible to everyone.
