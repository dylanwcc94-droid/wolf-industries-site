About Wolf Industriesand Design

Wolf Industries and Design is dedicated to creating professional, visually balanced layouts and innovative digital solutions. Our focus is on accessibility, responsive design, and user‑friendly experiences across all platforms.

Wolf industries and Design is a all in one company that specializes in creating unique and custom vinyl stickers as well as business cards, if you think it we will do our best to design it. The company is run and co-owned by a husband and wife team, it was started in the early months of 2021 and has been in the process of growing ever since. Dylan Oosthuysen a qualified electrician started the company by doing hand engraved night lights as a hobby and a few small electrical jobs, he was then joined by his wife Jolanda Oosthuysen who has her degree in Graphic and advertising design, who assisted him in the design of the night lights. ​ They started with the aim to provide a wide spectrum of services and strive to do so with professionalism, enthusiasm and dedication. One of their primary goals is to make the design process itself enjoyable for their clients and expand their creative ideas by making them a reality. Their focus for the future is to supply the business market in the region with their outstanding design solutions that would grow together the company and our clients success.

The company combines creativity with technical precision to deliver modern digital business cards, and design services tailored to client needs.

We believe in innovation, professionalism, and making technology accessible to everyone.
